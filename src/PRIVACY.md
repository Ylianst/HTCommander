# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 28, 2026

## Overview

HT Commander ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy
Policy explains how the App handles your information. In short: **we do not
collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.**

## Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect any personal or user data. Specifically, the App
does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Contacts
- Device identifiers or usage analytics
- Crash or diagnostic reports
- Any other personally identifiable information

## Permissions

The App may request certain device permissions (such as Bluetooth, microphone,
or location) solely to provide its core functionality — for example, connecting
to and communicating with a compatible radio. Any data accessed through these
permissions stays on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, because we do not collect any data.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted
in this file with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the
project's repository.
